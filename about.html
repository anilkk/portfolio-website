I'm originally from Finland but I moved to Berlin 3 years ago. Even though I long for the peaceful Finnish nature at times, I have no plans to leave Berlin. It's also here where I dared to make my career switch which I am very proud and excited about.

I believe that all my previous work experiences were meant to lead me into a career in UX Design. I have always been interested in human behavior and in understanding why people act the way they do.

I studied Economics and Marketing, whereafter I worked in online marketing for 5 years. My marketing background makes me an analytical and solution-oriented designer who strives to create enjoyable products where user and business needs meet.

I have a keen eye for detail and as a person I'm empathic and intuitive, and I'm always looking for meaning and connections in my everyday life. This allows me to deeply understand and empathize with people’s emotions, which I also see as one of my key strengths as a designer. I wish to design inclusive user experiences that bring meaning and joy to people’s lives.
