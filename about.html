About Katri

My story

I believe that all my previous work experiences were meant to lead me into a career in UX Design. I have always been interested in human behavior and in understanding why people act the way they do. I studied Marketing and I worked in online marketing (mainly SEO in the e-commerce field) for five years before I made the switch to UX Design. My marketing background makes me an analytical and solution-oriented designer who strives to create enjoyable products where user and business needs meet.

I have a keen eye for detail and as a person I'm empathic and intuitive, and I'm always looking for meaning and connections in my everyday life. This allows me to deeply understand and empathize with people’s emotions and feelings which I see as one of my key strengths as a designer. I wish to design inclusive user experiences that bring meaning and joy to people’s lives.
